Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

Dark Ascension is an offline idle/incremental game. Your privacy is important to us, so we want to be completely transparent: this app does not collect, transmit, or share any data whatsoever.

Data Collection

Dark Ascension does not collect any personal information or usage data. The app operates entirely offline with no network connectivity required.

Local Storage

Your game progress (save data) is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. If you delete the app, your save data is permanently removed.

Analytics & Tracking

Dark Ascension contains no analytics, telemetry, tracking pixels, or any other form of usage monitoring. There are no third-party SDKs, advertising frameworks, or data brokers integrated into the app.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with or transmit data to any third-party services. There are no ads, no in-app purchases beyond the initial purchase price, and no social features that require data sharing.

Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any data from any user, we also do not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated date. Since the app collects no data, we do not anticipate any material changes.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can open an issue on our support page.
